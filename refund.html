Refund Policy

We want you to be completely satisfied with your Virtual Smart Home experience. Here's our refund policy:

Pro Plan Refunds

If you're not satisfied with your Pro plan subscription, you can request a full refund within 14 days of your purchase. To request a refund:

  • Contact us through the contact form on our website
  • Include your account email and the date of purchase
  • We'll process your refund within 5 business days

Refund Process

Once your refund is approved:

  • You'll receive a confirmation email
  • The refund will be processed to your original payment method
  • It may take 5-10 business days for the refund to appear in your account, depending on your payment provider

Exceptions

Refunds may not be available in the following cases:

  • If the 14-day refund period has passed
  • If the account has been suspended due to violation of our terms of service
  • For free plan users (as no payment is required)
