Refund Policy

Last updated: March 2024

1. Refund Eligibility

We offer refunds under the following circumstances:

  • Technical issues preventing service usage
  • Service unavailability for more than 24 hours
  • Billing errors or duplicate charges

2. Refund Process

To request a refund:

  1. Contact our support team at support@virtualsmarthome.com
  2. Provide your account details and reason for refund
  3. Allow up to 5 business days for review
  4. If approved, refund will be processed within 10 business days

3. Refund Timeframe

Refund requests must be submitted within 14 days of the charge date. Refunds will be issued to the original payment method used for the purchase.

4. Non-Refundable Items

The following are not eligible for refunds:

  • Usage of service beyond 14 days
  • Custom development work
  • Third-party service fees

5. Partial Refunds

In some cases, we may offer partial refunds based on the unused portion of your subscription. This will be calculated on a pro-rata basis.

6. Subscription Cancellation

You may cancel your subscription at any time. Cancellation will take effect at the end of your current billing period. No refunds will be issued for the remaining period of an active subscription.

7. Contact Information

For any questions regarding our refund policy, please contact us at support@virtualsmarthome.com.
